Admission Rules
“The secret of all great undertakings is hard work and self-reliance.”
– Gustavus F. Swift
The school normally admits the students for Playgroup, Nursery, K.G. & Class I, (Age 2+ for play group, 3+ for Nursery & 4+ for KG.). However the students may be considered for admission to other classes as well, subject to the availability of seats and as per the norms laid down by the state government or C.B.S.E., which are as under:
  • A student seeking admission to any class in the school will be eligible for admission to that class only if he / she:
  • Has been studying in a school affiliated to C.B.S.E. or any other recognized Board of Secondary Education in India.
  • Has passed qualifying examination, making him / her eligible for admission to that class; Satisfies the requirements of age limits (minimum and maximum) as determined by the state government.
