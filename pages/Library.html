Security & Safety
“We cannot continue to rely only on our military in order to achieve the national security objectives that we’ve set. We’ve got to have a civilian national security force that’s just as powerful, just as strong, just as well-funded.”
-Barack Obama
Professionally trained security personnel watch over the totally fenced campus round-the-clock. Visitors are encouraged to seek prior appointment and provide proof of identity for authentication in order to enter the campus. The security personnel maintain continuous vigil on the movement of men and materials in and out of all installations and buildings with special attention on vital areas like the academic block. Besides, CCTV cameras are installed at all the strategic locations on the campus.
