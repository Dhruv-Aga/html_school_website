Facilities Digital Classrooms
‘If we teach today’s students as we taught yesterday’s, we rob them of tomorrow.’-
John Dewey
Simpkins School in equipped with digital classrooms. Digital classrooms brings a new dimension to using technology in the classroom. It is a comprehensive solution designed to assist teachers in meeting with their day to day classroom challenges and enhancing students’ academic performance with simple, practical and meaningful use of technology. Digital classrooms provide teachers with instant access to relevant instructor led multimedia content and instruction materials mapped exactly to the specific curriculum guidelines for use in class. It also enables teachers to instantly assess and evaluate the learning achieved by their students in class with innovative use of technology. Every class has its own Hi-tech multimedia computer with cordless key-board and cordless mouse enabled with a 6ft interactive white board which displays an enlarged image or video through powerful projectors and hi-fi speakers which can be seen and heard from every corner of the room. Digital classrooms provide many other benefits, such as, the classes are used now as a language lab, audio/visual lab, E-classes, internet equipped classes etc. Every class is also used as a library which has vast content of thousands of books and encyclopedia.
